Biography

Barlas Tan Özemek is a music producer, composer, arranger, and performing musician, and a graduate of the Music Department at Istanbul Bilgi University. His advertising music has been honored with "Best Advertising Music" twice at the Crystal Apple Awards and once at the Felix Awards.

Alongside film and advertising music composition, he works in mixing/mastering engineering and music supervision. As an artist, he is a founding member and musical backbone of the duo LALALAR, and has collaborated with artists such as Bülent Ortaçgil and Sezen Aksu.

His studio practice is built on Ableton Live, Avid Pro Tools, Logic Pro, and Cubase, with advanced performance skills on acoustic/electric/bass guitar. He is currently based in Berlin.
